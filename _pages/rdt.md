# Replication Design Team

The inaugural **REPLICATE-CER Replication Design Team** met in Charlottesville, Virginia, in June 2026 to collaboratively design the project's first multi-institution computing education research study.

Over two days of intensive discussion and planning, the Design Team developed the study's research question, designed the research protocol, identified common data collection procedures, and established the framework that will be implemented by Replication Team members during the 2026–2027 academic year.

The Design Team brings together computing education researchers representing research universities, master's institutions, and primarily undergraduate institutions from across the United States. Together, they developed the first REPLICATE-CER study and continue to support its implementation across participating institutions.

![REPLICATE Design Team](/assets/images/team_brainstorming.png)

---

## What the Design Team Accomplished

During the June 2026 workshop, the team:

- Developed the inaugural REPLICATE-CER research question.
- Designed a common multi-institution study protocol.
- Created shared data collection procedures.
- Established plans for cross-site analysis and dissemination.
- Developed materials to support faculty implementing the study during the 2026–2027 academic year.
