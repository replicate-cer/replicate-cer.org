# About

**REPLICATE-CER** supports foundational work toward **theory building in CER** by organizing **coordinated, multi-site replication studies** on open questions. Our model:

1. **Topic Selection & SLR** – choose a high-impact topic and publish a systematic review.
2. **Replication Design Team (RDT)** – ~5 researchers co-design a robust replication package.
3. **Replication Participants (RP)** – ~20 instructors/researchers run the study in their contexts.
4. **Baseline → Intervention → Meta-analysis** – standardized data collection and pooled analysis.
5. **Dissemination** – preregistration, de-identified datasets (as permitted), papers, and guidance.

We emphasize **equity-enabling** practices, recruiting participants across **institution types** (including MSIs, community colleges, and liberal arts institutions) to understand where and for whom interventions work.
