# Participate

We’re recruiting two kinds of contributors:

## Replication Design Team (RDT)
A small group (~5) with strong topic expertise who:
- Co-define research questions & dimensions of variation
- Specify common measures, instruments, and analysis plan
- Help mentor RP sites
- **Stipend:** $6,000 (per RDT member, per cohort)

## Replication Participants (RP)
A larger group (~20) of instructors/researchers who:
- Run the study in their course(s) using the replication package
- Collect baseline and intervention data
- Collaborate in monthly check-ins
- **Stipend:** $2,500 (per RP site, per cohort)

### What you’ll get
- A vetted **replication package** with ready-to-use instruments & IRB template
- **Monthly community** calls and 1-1 support
- **Co-authorship** opportunities on meta-analysis and experience reports
- Access to **analysis scripts** and shared infrastructure

> Interested? Email **info@replicate-cer.org** with your institution, course context, and interest area, or watch this site for our open call form (coming soon).
